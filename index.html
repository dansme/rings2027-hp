Rings and Factorizations 2027

The Conference on Rings and Factorizations 2027 is tentatively scheduled to take place from July 12–16, 2027 at the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics (FMF) of the University of Ljubljana in Ljubljana, Slovenia.

This meeting continues the traditional biennial series of ring theory conferences previously held in Graz in 2012, 2014, 2016, 2018, 2021, 2023, and 2025. It will be the first edition hosted outside of Graz, but otherwise follow the established format of our series. We hope to welcome many returning participants as well as new attendees in Slovenia in 2027.

More information will be provided on this website in due course.

Topics

The main topics include:
  • Multiplicative ideal theory: ideal and module systems, star and semistar operations,
  • Dedekind, Prüfer, Krull, and Mori rings and their generalizations,
  • Noncommutative rings and algebras,
  • Rings of integer-valued polynomials,
  • Topological aspects in ring theory,
  • Factorization theory in rings and semigroups,
  • Module theory and linear algebra over rings.
