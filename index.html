Rings and Factorizations 2027

The Conference on Rings and Factorizations 2027 will take place (tentatively) July 12th to 16th, 2027 at the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics (FMF) of the University of Ljubljana in Ljubljana, Slovenia.

This meeting continues the traditional biennial series of ring theory conferences previously held in Graz in 2012, 2014, 2016, 2018, 2021, 2023, and 2025. It will be the first installment taking place outside of Graz, but otherwise follow the usual format of our series. We hope to welcome many previous participants as well as new ones in Slovenia in 2027.

More information will appear on this website in due time.
